It is designed for:

- long-term conceptual understanding
- practical research reuse
- transfer into external systems such as OpenClaw
- stable growth without endless folder drift

The original `Code Archive` remains the historical store.
This archive keeps only what earns survival.

## How To Use This Archive

- Start with hub notes.
- Move from hubs into distilled briefs.
- Treat project lenses as proof of what has already been attempted.
- Treat strategic filters as decision boundaries, not optional commentary.
- Prefer links over duplication when a subject belongs in more than one place.

## What Belongs Here

A note belongs here if it does one of the following:

- clarifies a durable concept
- improves operational capability
- captures a reusable architecture
- sharpens judgment
- strengthens the larger research system

## What Does Not Belong Here

- weak fragments
- hype without operational consequence
- repetitive praise language
- notes whose only value was momentary excitement
