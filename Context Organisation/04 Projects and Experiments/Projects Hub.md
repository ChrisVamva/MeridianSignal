# Projects Hub

## Purpose

Projects are where the archive stops theorizing and starts testing.

They are preserved here not as raw history, but as reusable lessons.

## What Makes A Project Worth Preserving

- it reveals a recurring ambition
- it clarifies an architecture worth reusing
- it exposes a real bottleneck
- it shows how research becomes implementation

## What Gets Discarded

- temporary project noise
- partial notes that add nothing to the architecture
- implementation chatter without a reusable lesson
