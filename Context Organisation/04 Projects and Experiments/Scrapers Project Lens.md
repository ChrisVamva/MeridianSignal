# Scrapers Project Lens

## Project Purpose

The Scrapers Project translates research ambition into a market intelligence pipeline.

Its central question is:
How can data collection, evaluation, and content generation become a repeatable system?

## Why It Matters

This project captures an important transition in the archive:

- from reading about tooling
- to building an operational research machine

## Durable Value

### It proves the archive values automated evidence

The project is not only conceptual.
It shows a desire to gather external signals in a structured way.

### It connects agents, tools, and economic judgment

The project sits between technical execution and strategic decision-making.

### It shows where the research operating system meets the market

This matters because many notes in the wider archive are really about leverage, not just understanding.

## Lessons

- a good research pipeline needs clear phases
- data collection without evaluation logic is weak
- documentation becomes more valuable when it records what actually worked
