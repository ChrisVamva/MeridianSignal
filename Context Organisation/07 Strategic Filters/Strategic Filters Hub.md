# Strategic Filters Hub

## Purpose

Strategic filters preserve negative knowledge:

- what to distrust
- what to avoid
- what kinds of systems fail in predictable ways

## Why This Matters

A research system that only records positive discoveries becomes naive.
The archive's blacklist and cautionary notes are worth keeping when they are transformed into practical filters rather than theatrical complaints.

## Preservation Rule

Keep strategic filters when they change behavior.
Discard them when they are merely venting.
