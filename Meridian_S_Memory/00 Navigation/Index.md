# Agent_S_Memory

## Purpose

`Agent_S_Memory` is a prototype memory environment for agent-assisted research.

It is designed to help agents:

- understand the shape of your research
- see what is active right now
- preserve continuity across sessions
- generate useful next ideas without repeating settled ground

It is not a final constitution.
It is a structured line of sight.

## Relationship To Earlier Systems

- `Code Archive` is the historical archive.
- `Code Archive 2` is the curated research structure.
- `LUX` is the compressed agent-facing context prototype.
- `Agent_S_Memory` is the first attempt to combine durable research structure with live agent-readable memory.

## What This System Optimizes For

- research continuity
- clear orientation
- useful compression
- novelty through context
- human control over the deeper memory structure

## What This System Does Not Yet Optimize For

- rigid behavioral law
- autonomous self-editing
- finalized research identity
- fully specified agent roles
